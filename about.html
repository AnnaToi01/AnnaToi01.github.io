About Me

I'm a Master's student in Computational Biology and Bioinformatics at ETH Zürich, where I'm part of the top 3% of incoming MSc students with an ESOP scholarship. My research focuses on computational biology, bioinformatics, and machine learning applications in biological systems.

I have extensive experience in Python, R, and bioinformatics tools, with research internships at Roche, ETH Zürich, UZH, OIST Japan, and ISTA Austria. I'm passionate about understanding biological systems through computational approaches and developing innovative solutions in bioinformatics.
